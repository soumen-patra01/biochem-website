DYEING · LEVELLING & MIGRATION AGENT

Zetalev AB-55

Acid buffer for reproducible reactive dyeing on cellulose and polyester/cellulose blends

📋 Product Description

Zetalev AB-55 is an acid buffer that plays a crucial role in ensuring reproducibility during the dyeing of reactive dyes on 100% cellulose fibres. Its buffering effect maintains consistent pH levels, making it particularly beneficial when dyeing polyester/cellulose blends with disperse and reactive dyes. It is suitable for use at all stages of processing in HT one-bath processes on jets and circulating liquor machines. Specifically, it stabilises the dyebath pH as temperatures rise, counteracting the tendency for pH to shift towards alkalinity during the migration process. It effectively arrests bicarbonates and carbonates in the bath liquor, preventing undesired pH shifts before the addition of strong alkali for dye fixation. This controlled pH environment facilitates even dye fixation and enhances reproducibility. Additionally, Zetalev AB-55 possesses moderate dispersing and complexing properties, capable of sequestering positively charged earth metal ions present in the bath liquor, further contributing to process stability and quality outcomes.

✅ Key Benefits
  • Reproducibility Assurance

    Ensures consistent and reproducible dyeing processes, especially with reactive dyes on 100% cellulose fibres.

  • pH Stability Maintenance

    Maintains stable pH levels, crucial for dyeing polyester/cellulose blends with disperse and reactive dyes.

  • Bicarbonate & Carbonate Arrest

    Effectively arrests bicarbonates and carbonates in the bath liquor, preventing undesired pH shifts before alkali addition.

  • Dispersing & Complexing Properties

    Moderate dispersing and complexing action sequesters positively charged earth metal ions for improved process stability.

PRE-TREATMENT · DEMINERALIZING & SEQUESTERING AGENT

Zetaplex DM

Acidic demineralizing agent for pre-bleach heavy metal removal and peroxide protection

📋 Product Description

Zetaplex DM is a highly effective, acidic demineralizing agent specifically formulated for pre-bleach demineralisation treatment of cellulosic and synthetic fibres. Designed as a preparatory step before the bleaching process, it delivers powerful complexing and sequestering action against heavy metal ions including calcium (Ca²⁺), magnesium (Mg²⁺), iron (Fe³⁺), copper (Cu²⁺), and other metallic impurities present in process water, chemicals, and natural fibres.

Heavy metal contamination prior to bleaching is one of the leading causes of catalytic damage to hydrogen peroxide, resulting in fibre degradation, patchy bleaching, loose spots, pinholes, and reduced whiteness. Zetaplex DM neutralises these risks by effectively chelating and removing such impurities before the bleach bath is introduced. Its acidic character makes it especially effective in the pre-bleach acidification step, while its excellent compatibility with acids and common process chemicals ensures seamless integration into existing pre-treatment sequences.

✅ Key Benefits
  • Dedicated Pre-Bleach Demineralisation

    Specifically engineered as a demineralising step before bleaching — eliminates metallic impurities that would otherwise cause catalytic decomposition of hydrogen peroxide.

  • Prevents Catalytic Fibre Damage

    Removes iron and copper ions before the bleach bath, preventing catalytic attack on fibres — protecting against pinholes, holes, and fibre tendering.

  • Superior Heavy Metal Chelation

    Strong complexing action against Ca²⁺, Mg²⁺, Fe³⁺, Cu²⁺ and other heavy metal ions, keeping them in solution and preventing precipitation onto fibre or machinery.

  • Enhanced Bleaching Performance

    A well-demineralised fabric entering the bleach bath results in more uniform, brighter, and cleaner whites with consistent reproducibility across batches.

  • Scale & Deposit Prevention

    Prevents build-up of calcium carbonate, iron hydroxide, and other mineral deposits on machinery surfaces, reducing downtime and extending equipment life.

  • Threshold Sequestering Effect

    Maintains heavy metal ions in dissolved form even in supersaturated process liquors of CaCO₃, CaSO₄, BaSO₄, and Fe(OH)₃ at low dosage levels.

🔬 Applications & Dosage
Why demineralise before bleaching?
Fabric carries metallic impurities from feed water hardness, processing chemicals (salt, soda ash, caustic soda), and natural fibres. If not removed before the bleach bath, these cause: catalytic H₂O₂ decomposition → fibre damage & pinholes · uneven bleaching · reduced whiteness · scale deposits on machinery.

PRE-TREATMENT · SEQUESTERING & HEAVY METAL CHELANT

Zetaplex SQT CONC

High-temperature stable sequestering agent for bleaching, scouring, and dyeing processes

📋 Product Description

Zetaplex SQT CONC is a highly efficient, high-temperature stable sequestering agent specially engineered for textile wet processing. It delivers powerful chelation of heavy metal ions including calcium (Ca²⁺), magnesium (Mg²⁺), iron (Fe³⁺), copper (Cu²⁺), and other metallic impurities that originate from process water, chemicals, or natural fibres. These heavy metal ions, if left uncontrolled, lead to metal salt precipitation, lower whiteness in bleached fabrics, uneven dyeing, loss of dye yield, and poor reproducibility.

Zetaplex SQT CONC effectively eliminates these risks, ensuring consistent, high-quality output across both batch and continuous processing operations. Its anionic, water-soluble liquid formulation enables easy handling and precise dosing, while its broad compatibility with acids, alkalis, and other process chemicals makes it a reliable choice across all pre-treatment stages including demineralisation, scouring, and bleaching.

✅ Key Benefits
  • Superior Iron Chelation

    Provides high iron chelation properties, effectively controlling loose spots and pinhole problems that compromise fabric quality.

  • Excellent Demineralising Action

    Performs efficiently in both exhaust (batch) and continuous processes, removing hardness-causing ions and metallic impurities from process water and fibres.

  • Water Quality Independence

    Performance remains consistent regardless of variations in feed water quality or hardness levels, ensuring reliable and reproducible results.

  • Enhanced Whiteness in Bleaching

    When used in the bleach bath, delivers brighter and cleaner whites by preventing interference from heavy metal ions.

  • Suitable for Dyeing

    When used in the dye bath, helps achieve brighter, more uniform shades by preventing metal-induced dye precipitation and unlevel dyeing.

  • Threshold Sequestering Effect

    Maintains clarity in supersaturated solutions of CaCO₃, CaSO₄, BaSO₄, Fe(OH)₃ at very low dosage levels — keeping metal ions in solution even in the presence of precipitating agents.

  • Scale Prevention

    Eliminates scale build-up on bleaching machinery and process equipment, reducing maintenance downtime and extending machine life.

  • Reduced Ash Content

    Effectively lowers ash content on fibre substrates, contributing to cleaner and higher-quality finished goods.
