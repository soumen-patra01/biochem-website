Our Story

The Chemical Edge Your Mill Needs

Founded in August 2023, Biochem Technologies was built on a single conviction: Indian textile mills deserve better chemical solutions — better quality, better pricing, and better support — without having to choose between them.

We cover the full wet processing chain for both knit and woven segments: pre-treatment, dyeing auxiliaries, dyes, printing auxiliaries, and finishing auxiliaries.

No shortcuts on quality. No inflated margins. Just consistent performance that gives your mill a real competitive edge in a demanding market.

With offices in Kolkata and Ghaziabad, we serve mills across India with fast delivery and hands-on technical support whenever you need it.
